``` r
crab
```

## Format

A data frame with 41,228 rows and 14 variables:

- event.id:

  event.id

- trip.id:

  trip.id

- cfv:

  anonymized cfv

- dateland:

  date landed

- ctchdate:

  date caught

- latitude:

  latitude

- longitude:

  longitude

- year:

  year

- sw:

  standard week

- x:

  x coordinate

- y:

  y coordinate

- fleet:

  fleet

- gear.amount:

  gear.amount

- hours.fished:

  hours.fished
